About MountainView Group

MountainView Group is a holding company built to serve small, boutique, and medium businesses across South Africa. We believe that access to quality consulting, accounting support, and education should never be a barrier to growth.

📍 Our mission is simple: help businesses run better numbers and help students learn accounting — for free.

What we stand for

We are not a traditional corporate firm. We are built for accessibility, transparency, and long term thinking. Whether you are a small business owner trying to get your books in order, or a student struggling to understand debits and credits — we are here.

Our structure

MountainView Group is the parent company. Under it, MountainView Accounting provides bookkeeping and accounting administration support. MountainView Education offers free study materials, notes, and live sessions for accounting students.

The holding company itself also offers business consulting services — strategy, operations, and growth support for businesses ready to level up.

Who we serve

We work with small businesses, boutique agencies, and medium sized enterprises that need reliable financial and strategic support. We also serve students who are tired of expensive textbooks and want to learn accounting in a way that actually makes sense.
