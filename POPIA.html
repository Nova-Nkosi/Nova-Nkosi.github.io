1. INTRODUCTION & PURPOSE

MountainView Accounting (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This POPIA Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal information in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (“POPIA”).

We provide bookkeeping and accounting support. To do this, we must process personal information belonging to you, your employees, suppliers, and customers. This policy applies to all data subjects we interact with.

2. WHAT PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We only collect information needed to provide our services:

Special Personal Information: We may process ID Numbers, tax numbers, and financial info. We do not process race, religion, health, or biometric data unless legally required by SARS.

3. WHY WE COLLECT & PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION

We process personal information only for these lawful purposes:

  • Provide contracted services: Bookkeeping, PAYE/VAT prep, management accounts, CIPC filings
  • Legal obligations: Comply with Tax Administration Act, Companies Act, FICA
  • Legitimate business interest: Invoicing, client communication, debt collection, quality control
  • With consent: Marketing our services. You can opt out anytime

We do not sell your information or use it for unrelated purposes.

4. CONSENT

1. By signing our engagement letter and/or submitting our Client Intake Form, you consent to us processing personal information as set out in this policy.

2. Where we process employee/supplier info, you confirm you have their consent or another lawful basis to share it with us.

3. You may withdraw consent for marketing anytime by emailing MountainView.acc@outlook.com. Withdrawal does not affect processing needed for contracts or legal compliance.

5. HOW WE COLLECT INFORMATION
  • Directly from you: Engagement letter, intake form, email, WhatsApp, shared folders
  • From your records: Bank statements, payroll reports, invoices you upload to [Xero/Google Drive/Dropbox]
  • From third parties: CIPC, SARS eFiling only with your login or written instruction

7. INFORMATION SECURITY

We take reasonable steps to prevent loss, damage, or unlawful access:

  • Digital: 2FA on all accounts, strong passwords, encrypted laptops, reputable cloud software only. No client data on USB drives
  • Physical: Paper records locked away. Shredded after scanning
  • Access control: Only selected MountainView personnel + authorised contractors access data. All sign confidentiality agreements
  • Backups: Daily cloud backups of accounting data
  • Email: Sensitive docs sent via password-protected PDF or secure link, not plain email

Despite safeguards, no system is 100% secure. See clause 9 for breaches.

9. DATA BREACH PROCEDURE

If we become aware of a breach compromising your personal info:

  • 1. We will investigate and contain it immediately
  • 2. Notify you and the Information Regulator within 72 hours if your rights are at risk
  • 3. Provide details of info affected and steps to protect yourself
  • 4. Review security to prevent recurrence
10. YOUR RIGHTS AS A DATA SUBJECT

Under POPIA you may:

  • Request access: Ask what info we hold about you (Form 2). Free once per year
  • Request correction/deletion: Ask us to fix or delete info (Form 3). We must comply unless law says keep it
  • Object to processing: For marketing or legitimate interest (Form 1)
  • Lodge complaint: With us first: MountainView.acc@outlook.com Or with Information Regulator: complaints.IR@justice.gov.za

We will respond within 30 days.

11. CROSS-BORDER TRANSFERS

Some software servers are outside SA, e.g. Xero, Google. We only use operators in countries with similar privacy laws or contractual safeguards. By using our service, you consent to this transfer.

12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy for legal or operational reasons. Latest version always on request. Material changes notified via email.
